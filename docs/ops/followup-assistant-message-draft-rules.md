# Follow-up Assistant Message Draft Rules

## Purpose

This document defines draft message rules for the future AI Follow-up Assistant.

Message drafts are internal suggestions only. They must be reviewed by Paul before sending through Email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, phone, or any future external channel.

## Supported Languages

Future draft support may include:

- English
- Spanish
- Chinese
- mixed/translation later

Customer-facing export trade messages should normally use the customer's preferred language. Internal review notes may use Chinese.

## Supported Channels

Future draft channels may include:

- email
- WhatsApp
- LinkedIn
- phone script

Channel labels do not authorize channel integration or sending.

## Tone Styles

Supported tone styles:

- professional
- friendly
- concise
- technical
- quotation follow-up
- information request
- project clarification
- soft reminder

The tone should fit B2B foreign trade: clear, polite, specific, and not pushy.

## Message Structure

### Information Request

Recommended structure:

- greeting
- thank customer
- explain missing info
- ask specific questions
- explain why needed
- close politely

### Quotation Follow-up

Recommended structure:

- greeting
- reference quote/inquiry
- ask if reviewed
- offer clarification
- suggest next step

### Risk / Unclear Customer

Recommended structure:

- polite request for company/project information
- avoid giving sensitive pricing
- avoid delivery/payment/production commitment
- no internal risk label visible to the customer

## Required Safety Labels

Every draft should be marked:

- Draft only
- Not sent
- Human approval required

Recommended Chinese UI labels:

- 草稿
- 未发送
- 需人工确认

## What Drafts Must Not Do

Drafts must not:

- promise price
- confirm order
- confirm payment
- confirm production
- confirm delivery
- make legal commitment
- send automatically
- pressure customer aggressively
- reveal internal supplier/pricing data
- reveal internal cost, exchange rate, profit, or hidden quotation rules

## Example Draft Types

All examples below are examples/drafts only.

### First Response Asking For Missing Project Info

```text
Draft only - not sent - human approval required

Hello, thank you for your inquiry.

To check the most suitable aluminum profile solution, could you please share the project location, required quantity, finish/color, and drawings or photos if available?

With these details, we can review the specifications more accurately before preparing the next step.
```

### Quote Follow-up

```text
Draft only - not sent - human approval required

Hello, I hope you are doing well.

I wanted to follow up on the quotation we discussed. Please let me know if you have any questions about the specifications, quantity, finish, or packing requirements.

We will be happy to clarify the details before you make a decision.
```

### Request Company Website / Buyer Role

```text
Draft only - not sent - human approval required

Hello, thank you for your interest.

To better understand your project and prepare the right information, could you please share your company website and your role in this project?
```

### Spanish Basic Follow-up

```text
Draft only - not sent - human approval required

Hola, muchas gracias por su consulta.

Para revisar mejor los productos, podria compartir la cantidad requerida, el acabado/color y cualquier dibujo o foto disponible?
```

### WhatsApp Short Message

```text
Draft only - not sent - human approval required

Hello, this is a quick follow-up on your aluminum profile inquiry. Could you please send the drawings/specifications and target quantity when convenient?
```
