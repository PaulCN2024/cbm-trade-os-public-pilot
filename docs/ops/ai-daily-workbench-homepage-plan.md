# AI Daily Workbench Homepage Plan

## Purpose

The homepage should not be a passive dashboard. It should tell Paul what to handle today and help AI prioritize business work.

## Core Idea

Instead of showing only statistics, AI Daily Workbench should answer:

```text
保罗，今天最值得处理的是哪几件事？
```

## Dashboard Blocks

Planned blocks:

- today's priority tasks
- pending inquiries
- pending quotations
- pending supplier replies
- pending follow-ups
- high-risk quotes
- knowledge items needing verification
- new leads from AI Prospecting
- social/content drafts needing review
- customer country distribution
- quotation conversion metrics
- weekly new customers
- business bottleneck alerts

## Metric Cards

Include:

- customer total
- weekly new customers
- inquiry count
- quote count
- quote conversion rate
- pending follow-ups
- knowledge verification queue
- supplier response delays
- content drafts pending review

## AI Role

AI should:

- summarize today's work
- detect bottlenecks
- recommend next actions
- route Paul to the right module
- explain why a task matters
- show risk/source/confidence

## Safety

- no auto-send
- no auto-quote
- no auto-approve
- no automatic customer creation
- no business execution
- all actions remain draft/review/approval-first

## Visual Direction

- premium dark AI operations dashboard
- large metric cards
- daily priority panel
- conversion funnel
- country/product charts
- AI suggested actions
- clear read-only/approval-required badges
- original CBM Trade OS style

## Integration

Connect with:

- Customer Center

- BI
