# AI Quote Intelligence SKU And Price Rules Plan

## Purpose

Integrate quote system ideas into CBM Trade OS safely.

The goal is to make quote preparation faster and more explainable while preserving the existing rule that formal quotations, PI, price commitments, payment terms, and supplier/customer commitments require Paul approval.

## Features To Absorb

- quick quote
- one-sentence quote workbench
- quote records
- quote conversion tracking
- SKU management
- price rules
- customer quote history
- deal follow-up

## AI Role

- parse quote instruction
- identify products/SKU
- check price rules
- check exchange rate assumptions
- check FOB/tax/packing assumptions
- create quote draft
- mark missing info
- warn risk

## Safety

- no automatic formal quote
- no automatic PI
- no price commitment without Paul approval
- no supplier commitment without confirmation
- no sending
- draft only first

## Integration

- Quotation Pre-review
- Knowledge Center
- Supplier Intelligence
- Customer Center
- AI Command Center
- Approval Center later

## Implementation Phases

- QI1 planning
- QI2 static UI preview
- QI3 SKU/price rule data model plan
- QI4 read-only quote draft foundation
- QI5 quote calculation draft
- QI6 human approval
- QI7 formal quote/PI later
