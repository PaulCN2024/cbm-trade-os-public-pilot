# AI Customer Verification And Buyer Discovery Plan

## Purpose

Help Paul judge whether a customer/company is real, relevant, and worth following up.

## Customer Verification Checks

- company name
- domain/email match
- website existence
- country match
- business type
- product relevance
- social/media presence later
- suspicious signs
- duplicate customer check

## Buyer Discovery

- similar companies
- target country buyer types
- construction contractors
- facade contractors
- importers/distributors
- project owners
- product-specific buyer personas

## AI Role

- summarize company profile
- score fit
- flag risk
- suggest next follow-up
- recommend development angle
- connect to knowledge base and prospecting

## Safety

- no unauthorized scraping
- no private platform bypass
- no automatic outreach
- no risk score shown as absolute truth
- source/confidence required

## Integration

- AI Prospecting
- Customer Center
- Inquiry Center
- AI Daily Workbench
- Follow-up Assistant

## Implementation Phases

- CV1 planning
- CV2 static UI preview
- CV3 read-only verification records
- CV4 source confidence model
- CV5 follow-up draft integration
