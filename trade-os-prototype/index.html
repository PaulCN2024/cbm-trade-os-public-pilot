CBM Trade OS public demo

CBM Trade OS is a mock foreign trade business operating system for customer acquisition, lead management, inquiry analysis, project tracking, quotation drafts, order workflow, shipment tracking, after-sales service and repeat business development.

Current demo scope: independent website inquiry, action center, lead pool, customer 360, inquiry pool, mock AI summary, missing information analysis, project center, product library, quotation center, document center, follow-up tasks, order center, shipment center and after-sales center.

  • Business line A: architectural aluminum solutions, windows, doors, curtain wall systems, louvers, railings and ACP panels.
  • Business line B: precision aluminum manufacturing, CNC parts, extrusions with machining, brackets, housings and anodized or powder coated parts.
  • Safety rule: all official quotations, PI, price, delivery, payment and bank information require manual review.
  • Data rule: front CRM pilot data can come from Supabase after admin login; downstream commercial workflow remains mock localStorage data.

JavaScript enhances this page into the interactive CRM dashboard. If a crawler cannot run JavaScript, this fallback content is the readable overview of the deployed route.
